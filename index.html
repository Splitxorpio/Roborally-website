Where hardware and software comes together...


Join us on April 16th, 2023 for the exciting RoboRally competition at Circuit Launch in Oakland, where participants will showcase their creativity and technical skills by constructing remote-controlled cars using a combination of foam, 3D printing, and vex materials. In addition to assembly, they will incorporate motors and programming elements to ensure precise control over their vehicles. At the end, a race will be held to determine the fastest car among the participants.
